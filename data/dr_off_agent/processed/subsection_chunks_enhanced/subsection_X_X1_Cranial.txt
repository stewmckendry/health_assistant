A complex closure, repair and/or reconstruction is defined as surgical closure, repair and/or

a.for a lesion extending beyond the sella turcica; and

defect(s) and includes the harvesting and use of any autogenous materials and/or pedicled

reconstruction and/or repair.
2.E919 is eligible for payment, if rendered, when performed as part of a simple closure,
reconstruction and/or repair. E919 is not eligible for payment with E893.
[Commentary:

required, as defined in the note above.]
