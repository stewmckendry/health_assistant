CARDIOVASCULAR SURGICAL PROCEDURES
Cardiovascular Surgical ProceduresMarch 4, 2024 (effective April 1, 2024)
PREAMBLE
1. Unless otherwise stated, excision or repair procedures for arteries and veins include
endartectomy, thrombectomy and/or bypass graft.
2. Excision or repair procedures for arteries and veins include harvest of graft tissue, except where
harvest of graft tissue is explicitly excluded from the procedure. Where harvest of graft tissue is
included as a specific element of the procedure, the harvest is an insured service payable at nil.
